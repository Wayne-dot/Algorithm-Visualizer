Nonograms Algorithm Solver

Introdcution

Nonograms, also called Hanjie, Paint by Numbers, Picross, Griddlers, and Pic-a-Pix, among other names, are puzzles where you fill in or leave empty grid cells based on numbers at the edge to uncover a hidden image that looks like pixel art. For more information, click here

To summarize, you need to decide if the boxes needs to be fill or empty.
